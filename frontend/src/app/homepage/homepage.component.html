Site UID and RPUID Processing

The OSD DAIS system that generates Site UIDs and fadltty RPUIDs has been down for several weeks. At this time there is no projected date for the system to resume operation. Furthermore, when the system resumes operation, it may still not support the generation of Site UIDs and RPUIDs for ACES-RP.

The ACES help desk is aware of this situation, but are unable to provide a way forward at this time. Please refrain from calling, e- mailing or submitting help desk tickets to the FAS help desk for missing/unassigned Site UID and RPUID errors as that will simply cause delays in processing help requests that can be resolved. This outage does not affect the way ACES-RP operates for those sites that have already been issued Site UIDs and RPUIDs.

Those facilities awaiting RPUIDs are currently in the queue and will be processed as soon as DAIS interface is operational and AF business processes are in place. At that time the backlog of requests will be resubmitted and Site UID and/or RPUID requests will be assigned through ACES as appropriate. As soon as we receive notification that DAIS is operational for ACES-RP submission, you will be notified.

AFCEC and the ACES-RP team are painfully aware of the backlog this has and is continuing to create, but please understand that we do not control the DAIS interface; however, until further notice, please refrain from submitting help desk tickets for missing Site UID or RPUIID errors.
